Calculation controls

  • Additive cost = additive price × dosage / 1,000,000, rounded to two decimals before net and annual savings are calculated, matching the original calculator.
  • Specific energy = (main drive + ancillaries) / mill output. Power is assumed constant at the entered 2,000 kW.
  • Composition cost follows the original calculator: clinker, limestone and slag are costed; the hidden gypsum percentage remains in the mass balance, so clinker percentage is the residual to 100%, but gypsum is not costed separately.
  • CO₂ value = clinker fraction × clinker emissions factor × EUA price × exposure. This is a marginal opportunity-cost method.
  • Variable R&M follows the original calculator: scenario R&M per tonne = reference R&M × reference mill output ÷ scenario mill output. This converts fewer operating hours for the same production volume into a lower variable R&M cost per tonne.
  • Annual saving uses the same reference cement volume, derived from the annual clinker basis. Extra saleable tonnes from higher mill capacity are intentionally not monetized.
